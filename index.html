Hands-On How-Tos

Clear steps for classic VW tasks. Expand any topic to see the process.

Check for Spark: Coil → Distributor → Plugs
  1. Battery charged (≥12.4 V). Confirm clean grounds from battery to body and transmission strap.
  2. Key ON: verify +12 V at coil terminal 15 (positive).
  3. Crank: watch coil terminal 1 (negative) pulse (test lamp flashes / meter toggles).
  4. Pull center coil wire at the cap, hold ~6–8 mm from clean ground, crank—look for strong blue spark.
  5. If coil spark is good, check rotor, cap, and plug wires; then test plug spark at the electrode.
Static Timing (vacuum distributor)
  1. Rotate engine to TDC #1 (notch on pulley aligned; rotor points to #1 mark).
  2. Loosen distributor clamp. Turn ignition ON.
  3. Connect a 12 V test lamp between coil (–) and ground. Rotate distributor slowly until lamp just turns off/on at your target advance (e.g., 7.5° BTDC mark).
  4. Tighten clamp; recheck. Start engine and fine-tune with a timing light if available.
34 PICT-3 Baseline Settings
  • Idle speed screw: turn in until it just touches the stop, then ¼ to ½ turn further.
  • Bypass (big) screw: ~2½ turns out. Mixture (small) screw: ~2½ turns out (starting point).
  • Warm engine, adjust bypass for ~850–900 rpm; tweak mixture for highest smooth idle; re-set idle speed.
  • Spritz carb cleaner around base & boots to detect vacuum leaks (rpm change = leak).

Quick Tips

  • Point gap: 0.016 in (≈0.4 mm) on a 1600DP.
  • Spark plug gap: start around 0.024–0.028 in for many air-cooled setups.
  • Order: Firing order is 1-4-3-2, clockwise.
  • Grounds: Bad grounds mimic bad parts—clean and tight is king.

Always verify specs for your exact year/engine/distributor combo.

Tune-Up Card

Valve lash (cold): 0.006 in (intake & exhaust; many stock setups)

Point gap (1600DP): 0.016 in

Idle target: 850–900 rpm

Firing order: 1-4-3-2

Pulley Marks Helper

Wrap a paper strip around the pulley, mark circumference, then divide for degrees:

Degrees per mm depends on pulley diameter. Use a printable wheel or calculator.

Common advance marks: 7.5°, 10°, 30° (all-in check with light).

Charging Quick Check

Battery off: ~12.6 V (full). Engine at fast idle: 13.8–14.4 V.

If you see <13.5 V, inspect belt tension, regulator connections, brushes, and grounds.
